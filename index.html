What is Green Shop?

A Green Shop is a store that sells eco-friendly and environmentally safe products.

Why is a Green Shop important?

Because it helps protect the environment and reduces pollution.

What products are sold in a Green Shop?

Organic food, reusable bags, bamboo products, natural cosmetics, and recycled items.

How does a Green Shop help the environment?

It reduces plastic waste and encourages the use of reusable and recyclable products.

How is a Green Shop different from a normal shop?

A Green Shop sells eco-friendly products, while normal shops often sell plastic and chemical-based items.
